Kyushoshin Ju Jutsu

What is Kyushoshin Ju Jutsu?

Kyushoshin is a traditional martial art which focuses on improving your coordination, stamina, body control and knowledge in anatomy as well as Traditional healing Kuatsu. Kyushoshin follows the Old Ways about the teaching of technique, which are many and varied. It teaches:

  1. Jintai Kyusho - Vital points
  2. Koppo Jutsu - Bone breaking techniques
  3. Ju-Jutsu Nage Waza - Throws
  4. Kuatsu - Healing art of Ju-Jutsu
  5. Atemi Waza - Vital point strikes, nerve locks, and pinches
  6. Koryu Kata - Both armed and unarmed as they were originally taught
  7. Ne Waza - Ground techniques
  8. Jikiden Kaiden Sho - Weapons
    1. Ken (Sword)
    2. Bo (6ft staff)
    3. Hanbo (4ft staff)
    4. Tanjo (twin batons)
    5. Jo (single baton)
    6. Tanto (knife)
    7. Yubibo (finger staff)
    8. Nawa (rope)
    9. Tanbo (small staff)
    10. Wakizashi (short sword)
